Meet the Gaileys; Baking Life Great!

Located in Elko Nevada, Melanie and Jon Gailey launched their own baked goods business, Baking Life Great, in hopes of raising money to one day build a non-profit Women's Shelter. Building a home and career for themselves, and starting from scratch, they are determined to reach their goals and acheive their dreams.
